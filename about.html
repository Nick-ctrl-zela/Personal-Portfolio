About Me

Life is about balance. Like Thanos I appreciate that balance in all things. Unlike Thanos, I don't need to kill half the universe to feel like there is balance in my life, I simply do the following.

Kids

I have 3 of them, and when I'm not at work or in the coding class, I spend as much time as I can with them. They're only going to be this age once, and while they often want to do strange things and talk about stuff I often cannot understand, I still enjoy every moment I can with them. I do not live with any of them, so the time I have with them is just as precious as it is limited.

Video Games

When the kids are asleep or at their other parents' house and I'm all caught up on errands and homework, I get to play Overwatch 2. It's a rare thing that I get to play, but I enjoy the competitive nature as well as the inherit team centric system it encourages. I play support since I don't have the time to hone my mechanical skills, but my game sense and communication help me win games and climb the ranks despite little time played.

Hiking/Camping

This is the rarest form of fun I have because it takes the most amount of pre-planning and setup, but I truly appreciate getting away from civilisation and being in nature for a few days. Smelling the trees, listening to the birds, feeling the wind. Every simple earthly reminder that I'm alive and free and capable. That I exist along with my surroundings and cease to exist within them. I enjoy being separated from technology for that time and just disconnecting.
